A Stress Test

Are you feeling stressed? Do any of these high stress warning signs apply to you?

Loss of sleep
Feeling overwhelmed
Apathy, lack of energy.
Feeling on edge.
Being more emotional.
Using alcohol or drugs to relieve or forget stress.

Are you experiencing any of these symptoms?:

Headache.
Muscle tension or pain
Chest pain.
Stomach upset.
Sleep problems.
Fatigue.

Are you attempting to cope with your stress using any of these methods?:

Meditation.
Masturbation
Exercise
Drinking herbal tea
Yoga
Punching a punching bag
